# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection
This application does not collect, store, or process personal data.

## User Content
All text content processed by the App is provided directly by the user and is
written only into the user's own Canva designs.

The App does not retain copies of user content after the operation completes.

## Third-Party Services
The App integrates with Canva via its official API.
No analytics, tracking, cookies, or advertising services are used.

## Data Sharing
No user data is shared with third parties.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer
via the Support URL provided in the app listing.
